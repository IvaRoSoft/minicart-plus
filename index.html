PAYPAL CART WITH WEIGHT-BASED SHIPPING CHARGE

Minicart-Plus is a simple and free of charge Javascript PayPal shopping cart based on minicartjs.com where the total shipping fee is based on freight weight.

Would you like to start selling things on your website? Best is use PayPal secture servers for it. However, to allows your customers to complete their purchase and ultimately be re-directed to the PayPal you will need Minicart-Plus.

Minicart-Plus offers a simple and intitive shopping cart and few "buy now" buttons. Furthermore, Minicart-Plus offers a method to calculate shipping charges based on total weight. The formula for the shipping fee calculation is:

shipping_cost=shipping_fee_fixed+(shipping_weight_g/1E3)*shipping_fee_per_kg

So, at the end of the day, you can just use this code without really wondering how it works. You just need to set the weight for each item using "weight_g" and, the beginning of the code, define the following two variables:

shipping_fee_fixed = 12.00;

shipping_fee_per_kg = 6.50;

ADVANTAGES OF THIS CODE

Here some advantages of this shopping cart over others.

  1. Shipping fee is based on weight via a simple linear formula.
  2. Shipping fee is calculated and shown right away. Customers often leave your shopping page because unsure about shipping costs.
  3. Works on any browsers and device. The size of the shopping cart is reduced to allow people to buy from mobile phones and small devices.
  4. Shopping cart and the "My Cart" link is aways visible. Pretty cool...
  5. Written in JS, no need for server-side code. There is no need for any PayPal Business account.
  6. Minicart-Plus interfaces with PayPal for secure money transfer.
  7. It is FREE !
